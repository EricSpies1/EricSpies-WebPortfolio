About Me

I constantly watched videos on how to build a PC and couldn't wait to get one of my own. Once I acquired my own PC, I began to learn a lot about the Windows operating system. Over the years, I have gained a great amount of knowledge around PC parts and how to navigate and troubleshoot Windows OS. I now am taking courses at Ranken to pursue a career in Web & App Development.
